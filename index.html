Marriage Tradition

- Marriage is one of the important traditional values of family life in Vietnam. Traditionally, marriage is seen as a solemn ceremony and requires careful preparation from both families. - After the two families have agreed to get married, the wedding ceremony will be held. Traditional marriage in Vietnam, often have separate engagement rings and wedding rings. Engagement rings are usually simple rings, not too large and can be replaced with wedding rings after marriage. Wedding rings are often beautifully designed and are made from gold or silver. The wedding dress must be white, a symbol of the bride's innocence and purity. Weddings usually take place at home or at a restaurant. However, in recent times, many couples also choose to hold their wedding ceremony at other special locations such as on the beach, in the forest, in the mountains... The people who pay for the wedding are from the south, However, in recent times, many couples choose to hold their wedding and pay for the wedding by dividing the cost equally between the two families. The elders of the two families will conduct rituals such as prostration, hand-in-hand ceremony, ring-changing ceremony, and grassroots ceremony. After that, the groom's family will bring the bride home and conduct the entrance ceremony, ancestor worship and housewarming ceremony. In Vietnamese family life, the marriage traditions are still kept and respected, creating a close connection between the two families and also an opportunity for the younger generations to learn and continue the values. country's traditions.

Party

Wedding party is one of the important events in family life in Vietnam. Tradition often requires the party to be large and complete enough to show the success and affection of the two families. The menu often includes traditional dishes such as banh chung, fried spring rolls, grilled chicken, roasted pork and grilled seabass. These dishes are often beautifully arranged on the dining table, forming an attractive array of colors. Besides, alcohol is also an indispensable part of the wedding party in Vietnam. In addition to the food and drinks, the wedding party is also an occasion for relatives and friends to meet, chat and chat. It's great to share memorable moments in life with the people you love.
